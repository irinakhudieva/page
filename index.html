Hi-Tech house project

The project excludes unnecessary, flashy facades and materials. We build stone one- and two-story cottages in this style with a variety of layouts. You can choose a small project - from 200 sq.m., or you can choose a large-scale beautiful house of 1000 sq.m. m. with a swimming pool, rooms for servants, a library, a sauna, a hammam.

House in the power of hi-tech: stylish and modern

You will recognize Hi-Tech houses by their modern and somewhat futuristic design. The first thing that catches your eye is a flat roof, spacious terraces with glass parapets on the second floor. Clear lines, voluminous forms, panoramic glazing, the use of concrete, decorative plaster, planken in the exterior - these are the features of high-tech architecture that make this style recognizable.

The hi-tech cottage inside is an open plan, large spaces, floor-to-ceiling windows and exquisite quality materials in interior decoration. The location of the rooms is at the discretion of the customer. The first floor is near a residential, combined with a pedestrian, dining room. There can also be a guest room, study, SPA area, utility rooms.
The second floor most often becomes a sleeping area with dressing rooms, separate bathrooms for each family member. For children, you can visit the play area.

We have been designing and building them for 10 years and we know all the details. At the request of the customer, our company can erect a fence on the site, make the layout of the site and its improvement. Give us a call for more details or come to our office to discuss your future home.
